Live Session Guidelines (SOP)

Suggested Do's and Don'ts for effective live session delivery.

Full SOP document:↗ View SOP Doc
✅
Suggested Do's
Should join the session only using zoom app
Maintain clear audio with minimal background noise
Ensure a stable internet connection for smooth delivery
Use simple and easy-to-follow communication
Keep sessions engaging through Q&A, polls, or discussions
Try to address learner queries during or after the session
Have moderation support to manage chat and queries
Share recordings and session materials in a timely manner
Provide a brief recap or key highlights at the end
Include a feedback poll to capture learner experience
❌
Suggested Don'ts
Avoid background noise or distractions during sessions
Try to avoid connectivity issues that may disrupt the session
Avoid overly complex or difficult-to-follow communication
Avoid delays in sharing recordings or materials
